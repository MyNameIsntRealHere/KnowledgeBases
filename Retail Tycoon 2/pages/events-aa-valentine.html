Valentine Event

Annual Events

An event is an annually returning occurrence in RT2 where you can unlock decorations for your store and sell limited-time sellables.

Valentine Event

This event starts each year on the 9th of February and lasts untill the 20th.

For the duration of the event:
• You will be able to sell limited-time sellables: the Valentines Day sellables, including Teddy Bears, Heart Chocolats and Candy Bouquets.
• You can collect a total of 50 candy boxes that are spread around the map to unlock Valentine themed decorations for your store (which you keep even if the event ends).
