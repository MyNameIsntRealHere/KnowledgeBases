Employees

What do they do?

Employees help you automate your store. You can hire and fire employees via the employee menu at the top of the screen.

Employees level up when they work in your store, the higher their level, the quicker they can do their job.

Each of your employees gets a set salary per minute. The only time when you don't need to pay them is when your store is closed and all customers have left.
When you pause an employee, they will still take half of their salary.

There is no maximum amount of employees you can hire.
