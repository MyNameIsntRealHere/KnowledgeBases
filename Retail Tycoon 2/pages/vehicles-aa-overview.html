Vehicles Overview

What do they do?

Vehicles are used to transport goods from the loading dock to your store, or to simply drive or fly around the map. You can hire delivery drivers to do the transporting work for you, each delivery driver will need their own vehicle and a place to park at your store.

Where can I get them?

You can buy a new vehicle at Alan's Autos, located on the road above the loading dock.
