Vehicles

Quick Summary

You can buy vehicles to drive or fly around the map and to transport sellables.

Buying vehicles

You can buy vehicles at Alan's Autos, located on the road above the loading dock.

How to use them

With vehicles you can transport sellables from the loading dock to your store.

To automate this process you can hire a delivery driver who will drive or fly their assigned vehicle between your store and the loading dock.

Each delivery driver will need their own vehicle and a sufficient place to park at your store.

To see how much space you need for a vehicle, see that specific vehicle page.
