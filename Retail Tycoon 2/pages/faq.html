FAQ

Can't reach symbol

This icon means that one or more NPCs can't reach that place, so somewhere in your build the path from an NPC to that location is blocked. This blocked path can be anywhere on your plot, not only by this icon.

Here are some common issues that can lead to a blocked path:
• You used roof tiles as a flooring, try replacing that by a normal floor that is not a roofing tile.
• NPCs can only pathfind vertically and horizontally, NOT diagonally. Keep that in mind when designing your store.
• Shelving that is accessable via multiple sides (like the spinning car display) needs to be accessable via all sides at all times. Just having one side open is not enough.
• There may be one or more NPCs trapped in a (enclosed) room or a customer in an employee-only zone.

My manager is not buying any sellables

The usual issue is that the sellables are waiting at the loading dock, ready to be picked up by you or a delivery driver.

Even when you have your manager on instant delivery it is still worth checking the loading dock to see if your manager ordered the items from a time you didn't have instant delivery turned on.

My manager is occupying my finance reps desk and visa versa

Try replacing your desks with employee-specific desks instead of the any-employee desk you have (accidentally) placed now.

It can be confusing since the any-employee desk looks the exact same as the finance rep-only desk.

My janitor is complaining about trash bins, even when I have placed multiple

Your janitor is not asking for a trash bin, but for a dumpster. You can find this in the infrastructure tab in build mode.

This is often a simple translation error.

I am unable to remove lights and other ceiling items in build mode

In order to remove lights, you will need to navigate to the ceiling tab in build mode and then use the remove tool to remove them.

My walls are gone when loading my save / Some items didn't load in when loading a save

If you run into this issue, it means that your store couldn't completely load in.
Try joining another (less busy) server.

Try playing in a private server by yourself to avoid this issue.

My money trees are not giving me the correct amount of extra money

Money trees give a 2% increase on the PROFIT of items, not on the selling price.

(profit = selling price - buying price)
