Gamepasses

What is it?

With gamepasses in RT2 you can pay robux to unlock extra content or customization for your store.

Overview of all gamepasses

GamepassPriceUnlocks
Custom RadioR$149Allows you to make custom music playlists to play in your store
Custom FloorsR$149Allows you to have custom floor textures
Custom Color PalettesR$299Allows you to make custom color palettes for most buildables
Electric SemiR$299Unlocks the electric semi
More LandR$399Allows you to buy extra land for your store to build on
Military ClearanceR$499Unlocks military vehicles, sellables and decorations
