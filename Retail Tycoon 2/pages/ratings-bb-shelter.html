Shelter

What is based on?

Your customers want a roof above their head and a wall to all 4 directions around them. If that is achieved you get 5 stars.

Walls and roofs everywhere, still not 5 stars?

Your customers start giving out ratings the moment they arive in your store. That means that your parking lot also contributes to your ratings and requires some sort of shelter.
