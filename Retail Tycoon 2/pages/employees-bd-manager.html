Manager

Function

A manager is a type of employee you can hire to work in your store.

They help you by ordering sellables. You can configure the amount to stock and whether or not you want to use instant delivery.

Each time they level up, their spending limit goes up by 10% (level 5: 40% higher spending limit).

Amount to stock

The amount to stock option tells your manager how much stock they need to buy:
• 50%: Your manager will order as much items that are needed to fill all your shelves half full.
• 100%: Your manager will order as much items to fill all your shelves fully.
• 150%: Your manager will order as much items to fill all your shelves fully + have as much items in storage to fill all your shelves for 50% again.
• 200%: Your manager will order as much items to be able to fill all your shelves twice.

Having multiple managers set to 200% won't make them stock to 400%, they will work together to reach the goal of 200%.

Instant delivery

You can choose to enable the option "instant delivery". This will increase the price of the sellables your manager buys by 25%, but they will go directly into your stores storage, eliminating the need to transport them from the loading dock to your store.
