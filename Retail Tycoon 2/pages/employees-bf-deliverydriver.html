Delivery Drivers

What do they do?

A delivery driver is an employee you can hire to work in your store.

They drive/fly between your store to transport goods from the loading dock to your store. In order to do this they need a vehicle and a sufficient place to park see the vehicles page.

Each time they level up, they drive 5% faster (level 5: 20% faster).
