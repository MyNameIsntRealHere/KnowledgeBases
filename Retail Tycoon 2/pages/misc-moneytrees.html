Money Tree

Function

A money tree is a special item you can place in your store to increase your profits.

Every money tree you have placed in your store will increase profits by 2%.
(profit = selling price - buying price)

You can see how many money trees you have by looking at the number next to the money tree in build mode (decorations tab), if you don't see a money tree there you have not earned one yet.

Obtaining

You can obtain money trees by reaching milestones by selling a set number of sellables in your store.

You can find the milestones in the settings menu.

Each time you reach a milestone, you will be rewarded with 1 money tree and the milestone will increase so you can earn multiple money trees for each sellable.
