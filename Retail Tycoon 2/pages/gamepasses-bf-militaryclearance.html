Military Clearance

Function

Military Clearance is a gamepass for Retail Tycoon 2 costing 499 robux.

This gamepass unlocks:
• Military truck and bradley as driveable vehicles. Each save you start will have both vehicles in your inventory for free, and you have the option to buy more at Alan's Autos.
• New sellables: Guns, Ammo, Military Vehicles and Armored Vehicles.
• New builing items: Armored Cabinet, Wired Fence.
