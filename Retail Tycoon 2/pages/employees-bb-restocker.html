Restocker

Function

A restocker is a type of employee you can hire to work in your store.

They help you by restocking shelves in your store.
You need to assign sellables to shelves in your store by using restocking mode at the top of your screen (see image).

Each time they level up, they walk 15% faster (level 5: 60% faster).
