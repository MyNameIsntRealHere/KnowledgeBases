Annual Events

An event is an annually returning occurrence in RT2 where you can unlock decorations for your store and sell limited-time sellables.

Christmas Event

This event starts each year somewhere around the beginning of December.

For the duration of the event:
• You will be able to sell a limited-time sellable: the Christmas Tree
• You can collect a total of 50 presents that are spread around the map to unlock Christmas decorations for your store (whcih you keep even if the event ends)
• The map will stay in the winter season the whole time
• Some customers will come with their sleigh instead of their car.
