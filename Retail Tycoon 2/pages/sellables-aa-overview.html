Sellables

Sellables (or products) are the items you sell to your customers.

How to get them to your storage?

You can order sellables via the storage menu at the top of your screen, in here you can see the buying price, selling price and unit size of each product and how much of them you have in your storage.
You can hire a manager to automate the ordering process.

Once you ordered the sellables, you (or your delivery driver) will need to pick them up at the loading dock and transport them to your store.

You can also choose the option "instant delivery" when ordering, this will teleport the sellables directly to your storage instead of to the loading dock. Using this will increase the buying price by 25%, without increasing the selling price, making less profit and some sellables even making a loss. (this does not affect money trees)

Money Trees

Money trees give you a 2% increase on all profit you make by selling sellables.

You can earn money trees by selling a certain amount of sellables in your store. You can find these milestones when you scroll down in your store statistics. Once you hit a milestone you unlock a money tree and the milestone increases so you can earn another money tree for that item. (You need to place the money trees on your plot in order to let them work. There is no max amount of money trees)

profit = selling price - buying price

Deleting Sellables

You can remove sellables from you inventory by going to the storage menu at the top of the screen and selecting the trash button instead of the Buy button.
