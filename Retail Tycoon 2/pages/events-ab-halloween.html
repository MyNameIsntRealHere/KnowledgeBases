Halloween Event

What's an event?

An annual event in Retail Tycoon 2 is an automated update that takes place every year for a short period of time. During this time, you can sell limited-time products and you can collect certain items around the map to unlock themed decorations.

The products you buy stay in your inventory when the event has ended, so you can stock up as much as you want!
The content you unlock by collecting items around the map stays usable even after the event has ended.
Each year the same content returns to the event so you won't unlock anything extra if you go around the map and collect the items again.

Halloween Event

During the halloween event, you can smash 50 pumpkins around the map to unlock halloween decorations (Spooky Railing, Cobwebs, Jack-O-Lanterns and halloween-themed Hanging Decorations).
There are also limited-time halloween items available to sell in your store: Jack-O-Lanterns, Lanterns and Candy Buckets.
