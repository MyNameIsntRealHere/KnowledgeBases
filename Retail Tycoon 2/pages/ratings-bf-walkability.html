Walkability

What is based on?

Your customers don't want to walk on a too complex path to get to where they need to be. They like to walk in straight lines rather than taking lots of turns.

The third overlay in build mode is NOT to view the walkability rating in your store. That overlay helps you with your crowdedness rating.
