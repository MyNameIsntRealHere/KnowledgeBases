Finance Rep

What do they do?

A finance rep is an employee you can hire to work in your store.

They give out loans to your customers so they can buy more expensive items. The more money a customer wants, the lower the chance they get the loan given.

The money you give out does not come from your own money.

Each time they level up, their work speed goes up by 10% (level 5: 40% faster).

Minimum loan

In the employee settings you can set a minimum loan. This is the minimum amount of cash a customer needs in order to be able to see a finance rep.

This comes in handy if you don't have enough money for enough finance reps yet. If you set the amount for example on $500, customers that want to buy a car will be able to see a finance rep, but people that miss 2 bucks to buy another banana will not be able to see a finance rep.
